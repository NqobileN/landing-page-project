Cybersecurity in Africa

Cybersecurity - When one thinks of cybersecurity, images of hackers, phishing attacks, online scammers, etc pop up. They would not be wrong as cybersecurity works to mitigate such attacks on an individual. However, have you ever thought of cybersecurity at state-level? How does it work, and what effect does it have on said state?

Well, cybersecurity plays a crucial role at state-level, more in particular, within politics. Within African states, cybersecurity plays a crucial role at ensuring National Security, Election Integrity, International Relations and Cyber Diplomacy, as well as the mitigation of Information Warfare and Disinformation.
